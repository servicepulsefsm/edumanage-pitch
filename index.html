The Problem

A parent pays fees. Sends their child to school. And then… silence.

What happened in class today? They don’t know. They ask the child — “it was fine.” They ask other parents — everyone has a different story. They wait for the term report card — a single sheet of paper, three months later.

The relationship between parents and schools runs on hearsay, delayed reports, and trust-by-default.

AI With ROI

AI that saves money — not just time

Every feature here has a quantifiable dollar value. This is how EduManage pays for itself.

Facial Recognition Attendance

Teacher takes one class photo. AI identifies every face. Attendance done in 3 seconds, not 10 minutes. Saves 100–200 teacher-hours per year. Parents get instant “your child is in class” WhatsApp confirmation. Auditable. Fraud-proof.

AI Report Card Narratives

Teachers spend 3 weeks per term writing the same generic comments. EduManage writes personalised narratives from the 360 Passport: “Tendai improved 15% in Mathematics, particularly geometry. Participation is up. Written expression needs focus.” 3 weeks → 2 hours.

AI Lesson & Assessment Creator

Not generic ChatGPT. AI that knows YOUR syllabus position, YOUR students’ weak topics, YOUR curriculum (ZIMSEC/CAPS). Generates lesson plans, assessments, and revision material calibrated to where the class actually is.

Smart Fee Recovery

AI predicts which families will default on fees. Sends personalised payment plans via WhatsApp: “4 × $50 instead of $200.” Improving collection by even 10% = thousands recovered per term. The #1 survival metric for private schools.

Revenue Growth

The features competitors forgot

Every competitor manages current students. Nobody helps the school grow.

Enrollment Pipeline

Track every inquiry from first phone call to enrolled student. Conversion rates, lead sources, dropout points. No competitor offers this. Schools spend on marketing but have no visibility into what converts.

Alumni Loop

Track where graduates go — university, employment, careers. “85% of our graduates went to university” is the most powerful enrollment pitch a school can make. The 360 Passport makes this automatic.

Family Intelligence

Every competitor treats students individually. But families decide together. If one child is unhappy, ALL children leave. Losing one family of 3 = $6,000–9,000/year. EduManage sees the family, not just the student.

Parent NPS via WhatsApp

Monthly, automatic: “How satisfied are you with [School]? Reply 1–10.” Aggregate = Parent Confidence Score. Trend over time = early warning. Free to send. Worth thousands in retained students.

The school owner’s question is never “does it have features?” It’s “will it help me keep students, attract students, and collect fees?” EduManage answers all three.
